About us


Our purpose is to spread awareness about physical health and help improve it for our users. We help users discover new exercises, keep track of their progress, set higher goals for themselves, and recommend new challenges to improve their bodies. Furthermore, we provide a platform where users can interact with each other, encouraging and giving each other advice. Our team has been continuously looking for ways to improve user experiences by reading the feedback people give us. If you want to help out, joining our communities linked below will be much appreciated.
